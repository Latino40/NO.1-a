Admission Process

We welcome applications from students who demonstrate academic potential and a commitment to our school values. Follow these steps to join Hosanna Secondary School:

1

Application

Complete the application form available below or at our school office.

2

Entrance Exam

All applicants sit for an entrance examination in English and Mathematics.

3

Interview

Shortlisted candidates and parents attend an interview with the admissions panel.

4

Admission

Successful candidates receive admission letters and complete registration.

Admission Requirements

New Students (S1)

  • Completed Primary Leaving Examination (PLE)
  • Original PLE results slip
  • Birth certificate
  • 4 recent passport photos
  • Transfer letter from previous school (if applicable)

Transfer Students

  • Latest school report card
  • Transfer letter from previous school
  • Birth certificate
  • 4 recent passport photos
  • Recommendation letter from previous school

Scholarships & Financial Aid

Hosanna Secondary School offers several scholarship opportunities for outstanding and needy students:

Academic Excellence Scholarship

For students with exceptional PLE results (Aggregate 4-8). Covers up to 100% of tuition fees.

Talent Scholarship

For students demonstrating exceptional ability in sports, music, or arts. Covers 25-75% of fees.

Need-Based Scholarship

For academically promising students from disadvantaged backgrounds. Amount varies by need.

To apply for scholarships, submit the scholarship application form along with supporting documents.
