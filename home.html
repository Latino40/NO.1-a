Welcome to Hosanna Secondary School

At Hosanna, we are committed to providing quality education that nurtures the holistic development of our students. Our dedicated staff and modern facilities create an environment where students can thrive academically, socially, and spiritually.

Our Mission

To provide quality education that empowers students to achieve their full potential academically, spiritually, and socially through innovative teaching methods and a supportive community.

Our Vision

To be a center of academic excellence that produces well-rounded individuals who positively impact society through leadership, innovation, and service.
