Academic Departments

Sciences Department

Our science department is equipped with modern laboratories and experienced teachers who make learning interactive and practical. We emphasize hands-on experiments and real-world applications of scientific principles.

Humanities Department

The humanities department fosters critical thinking about human society, culture, and history. Our approach connects classroom learning with current events and community issues.

Languages Department

We develop strong communication skills in multiple languages, preparing students for global citizenship. Our language programs include cultural immersion activities and exchange programs.

Co-curricular Activities

At Hosanna, we believe in holistic education. Our co-curricular program complements academic learning with opportunities for physical, creative, and social development.
